Steak

Description

Simple grilled steak.

Ingredients

  • Rib-eye steak
  • 2 tablespoons sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon pepper
  • 2 cans of beer of choice

Steps

  1. Place the steaks in a large, shallow container with a lid. Season each side of the steaks with the salt and lemon pepper. Gently pour the beer over the steaks (making sure the seasoning doesn't wash off). Cover, and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours.
  2. Preheat grill for high heat.
  3. Preheat grill for high heat. Lightly oil grill grate. Place steaks on grill, and discard beer marinade. Cook for 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
